Nice to meet you.


My name is Emily and I am a multidisciplinary designer from Winnipeg, Manitoba with over seven years of experience crafting designs that make an impact. From branding and web design to motion graphics and photography, I love bringing creative ideas to life across print and digital mediums. I’ve had the pleasure of working with clients like Visa, Oracle, the Royal Winnipeg Ballet and True North Square, always aiming to deliver designs that not only look great but also align with strategic goals. With a strong foundation in design tools like Adobe Creative Suite and a knack for juggling multiple projects, I’m all about creating meaningful visual experiences that resonate with audiences and tell powerful stories. When I'm not in front of my laptop you can usually find me at café, crocheting or fire dancing.
