Verde at Home

During the COVID-19 pandemic, the Verde Salon experience was reimagined, bringing luxury hair treatments straight to your doorstep. This game-changing service offered personalized consultations, high-quality products, custom-formulated treatments, and everything neededed for flawless, salon-worthy hair—all in the comfort and safety of your own home.


Deliverables

Packaging, Print, Strategy, Web Design, E-commerce, Social Media, Photography, Videography, Illustration


The Objective

To maintain brand loyalty and drive sales by adapting to the unique challenges presented by the COVID-19 pandemic.


Our Solution

To create a seamless, luxurious experience that brought Verde Salon directly to clients' homes. We designed custom packaging and illustration that captured the brand’s premium vibe and built an easy-to-navigate e-commerce platform for effortless shopping. With a mix of beautiful photography, engaging videography, and creative social media content, we told the story of "Verde at Home" in a way that felt personal and inviting—helping clients feel connected to the brand and confident in their at-home hair care journey.
